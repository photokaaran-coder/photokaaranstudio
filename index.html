About Photokaaran

Photokaaran Studio was established in 2002 with a single vision — to capture life's most beautiful moments with artistry and authenticity. Based in the heart of Chennai, we've grown into one of the city's most trusted photography and videography studios.


Under the leadership of our proprietor Premnath, we've delivered world-class photography services across Chennai and worldwide. With over two decades in the business, our passion for storytelling through images has never wavered.
